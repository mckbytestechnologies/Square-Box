Frequently Asked Questions

What types of maintenance issues can I report?
You can report any maintenance issues related to your property, including plumbing, electrical, HVAC, structural issues, appliance malfunctions, and more. For emergency situations, please call our emergency line directly.
How long does it take to resolve a maintenance request?
Response time depends on the urgency of the issue. Emergency requests are addressed within 24 hours, urgent requests within 48 hours, and standard requests within 5-7 business days.
Is there a cost for maintenance services?
It depends on your warranty or maintenance plan coverage. Some repairs may be fully covered, while others might have a service fee. We will always provide a cost estimate before proceeding with any work.
