Frequently Asked Questions

Is there any cost to list my property?

Listing your property on SquareBox is completely free. We only charge a success fee once your property is successfully sold or rented through our platform.

How long does it take to list a property?

Our streamlined process takes just 10-15 minutes to complete your listing. Once submitted, our team will verify and activate your listing within 24 hours.

Can I update my listing after publishing?

Yes, you can easily update your listing anytime through your dashboard. You can change photos, description, price, and other details as needed.

How do you help me determine the right price?

Our pricing tool analyzes similar properties in your area and current market trends to suggest an optimal price range. Our experts can also provide a free valuation.
