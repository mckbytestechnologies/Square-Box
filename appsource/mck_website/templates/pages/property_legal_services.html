Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our legal services for real estate.

What documents are required for property verification?
Typically, we need the title deeds, encumbrance certificate, property tax receipts, building approval plans, and any existing agreements related to the property.
How long does the due diligence process take?
The due diligence process usually takes between 7-10 working days, depending on the complexity of the property documents and the responsiveness of local authorities.
Do you provide services for NRIs?
Yes, we have extensive experience assisting NRIs with their real estate legal needs in India, including power of attorney documentation and remote property management.
What is the cost of your legal services?
Our fees depend on the specific services required and the complexity of the transaction. We offer transparent pricing and will provide a detailed quote after understanding your requirements.
